About klaculate
klaculate was created as a useful and informative calculator. Its purpose is to teach the basic order of operations for simple math problems, showing each individual step along the way to a correct answer.
Even though klaculate is a CaaS (calculator-as-a-service), it is open source. Improvements and even whole new features are accepted, simply send a PR. Please report any errors you encounter.
  • Open-Source: https://github.com/LadySerenaKitty/klaculate
  • Error Reporting: https://github.com/LadySerenaKitty/klaculate/issues
  • Sending Patches: https://github.com/LadySerenaKitty/klaculate/pulls
klaculate runs on FreeBSD
